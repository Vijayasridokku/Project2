Films Data Set

  • The Films dataset reveals insights that are typically difficult to identify. Tableau helps visualize these insights clearly.
  • The Drama genre has won the highest number of awards compared to other genres.
  • Wayne John, Clint Eastwood, and Paul Newman are the top three actors with the most popularity.
  • The number of films produced each year has increased drastically over time.

Cars Data Set

  • America has the largest share of car manufacturers, followed by Europe and Japan.
  • Ford is the manufacturer that has sold the highest number of cars over the years.
  • The displacement (distance traveled by cars) has reduced over time.
  • Car manufacturing remained constant in European nations but declined in the U.S. after 1974.

Cereals Data Set

  • The dataset includes manufacturers A, G, K, N, P, Q, and R. The highest manufacturer is K, while A has the lowest production.
  • Vitamin levels in cereals range from 0 mg, 25 mg, to 100 mg. Six cereals contain 100 mg of vitamins: Total Whole Grain, Total Raisin Bran, Total Corn Flakes, Just Right Fruit & Nut, Just Right Crunchy Nuggets, and Product 19.
  • Fiber content in cereals varies, with the highest fiber content found in "All-Bran with Extra Fiber."
  • Sodium levels in cereals have generally increased in newly released products.
